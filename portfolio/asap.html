Causes of Water Damage

Water damage can occur for a variety of reasons, but some common causes include: Roof Leaks: A leaky roof can allow moisture to seep into your attic, walls, or ceiling, promoting mold growth. Left untreated, roof leaks can lead to structural damage.

Preventing Future Mold Growth

To prevent future mold growth, it’s crucial to repair water damage promptly. Address any roof leaks, fix burst pipes, and ensure proper drainage around your property. Regular inspections of high-risk areas like attics, basements, and bathrooms can help catch problems early.
